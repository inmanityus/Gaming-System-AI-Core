## üîÑ CORE GAMEPLAY LOOP: "Harvest & Haggle"

### Stage 1: The Order (Shopping List)
**Location**: The Abattoir (Dark World marketplace)

**How It Works**:
- Receive orders from Dark World clients
- Low-tier: "50kg raw flesh, any quality" (public bounty)
- High-tier: "One O-negative athletic female, heart pre-mortem" (private encrypted request)
- Orders dictate Human World targets

### Stage 2: The Hunt (Acquisition)
**Location**: Human World

**Method Matters**:
- ‚ùå **Shotgun blast**: Fast but ruins organs ‚Üí "Junk" quality
- ‚úÖ **Blade/poison**: Quiet, preserves parts ‚Üí "Good" quality
- ‚úÖ‚úÖ **Live extraction**: Difficult but best ‚Üí "Prime/Pristine" quality

**Targets**:
- Surgeon path: Criminals, traffickers, abusers
- Butcher path: Anyone (hospitals, schools, suburbs)

### Stage 3: The Harvest (Processing)
**Location**: Workshop (upgradable: ambulance ‚Üí club backroom ‚Üí corporate lab)

**Mechanics**:
- Tense skill-based mini-game
- **Timer**: Decay (body parts have shelf life)
- **Tools matter**: Rusty hacksaw vs surgical laser
- **Skill matters**: Botch = worthless mush, Success = "Pristine" quality

### Stage 4: The Haggle (The Sale)
**Location**: Dark World client meetings

**Mechanics**:
- Dialogue-based negotiation
- Factors: Reputation, quality, understanding client personality
- **Fail low-tier**: They might attack/eat you
- **Fail high-tier**: Blacklisted OR bounty on YOUR head

## üèöÔ∏è DARK WORLD CLIENTELE (8 Tiers)

### **LOW-TIER** (Street-Level Scum)

**The Carrion Kin** (Ghouls & Scavengers)
- **Buy**: Bulk flesh, bones, blood (not picky)
- **Territory**: Dark World slums
- **Payment**: **"Grave-Dust"** - Gritty stimulant (strength + pain resistance, causes paranoia)
- **Risk**: Might eat you if hungry

**The Chatter-Swarm** (Insectoid Hives)
- **Buy**: Eyes, tongues, vocal cords (grant speech/sight to drones)
- **Communication**: Clicks and stolen whispers
- **Payment**: **"Hive-Nectar"** - Jelly (telepathic communication with crew)
- **Risk**: Hive-mind can overwhelm you

### **MID-TIER** (Gentry & Guilds)

**The Stitch-Guild** (Flesh Golems & Necro-Artisans)
- **Buy**: Pristine skin (upholstery), long bones (framework), nervous systems (constructs)
- **Quality**: Meticulous, reject anything imperfect
- **Payment**: **"Still-Blood"** - Alchemical regeneration agent (very valuable)
- **Risk**: Guild politics, rival artisans

**The Moon-Clans** (Were-beasts)
- **Buy**: Specific organs for rituals (brave hearts, drinker livers, runner lungs)
- **Tradition**: Shamanistic, honor-based
- **Payment**: **"Moon-Wine"** - Inhuman speed + senses (risk: primal fury)
- **Risk**: Pack mentality, territorial conflicts

**Vampiric Houses** (House Volkov, House LaSombra, etc.)
- **Buy**: Blood by type, purity, "vintage" (lifestyle matters)
- **Connoisseurs**: CEO blood = bland, athlete blood = "vibrant"
- **Payment**: **"Vitae"** - Hypnotic charm + influence drug
- **Risk**: House politics, vampire social hierarchy

### **HIGH-TIER** (Ancient Powers)

**The Obsidian Synod** (Ancient Golem-Minds)
- **Buy**: Brains + spinal columns of geniuses (scientists, programmers, strategists)
- **Purpose**: Integrate into "Great Thought" network
- **Payment**: **"Logic-Spore"** - Crystal drug (see data-streams, predict movements, impossible calculations)
- **Risk**: They don't value your life, only your utility

**The Silent Court** (The Fae)
- **Buy**: CONCEPTS, not parts (child's laughter, hero's dying breath, traitor's guilt)
- **Method**: Harvest emotions/moments at precise times
- **Payment**: **"Enchantments"** - Permanent reality-bending perks (always ironic twist)
- **Risk**: Fae wordplay, binding promises, eternal debts

**The Leviathan Conclave** (Abyssal Entities)
- **Buy**: SOULS (live delivery required)
- **Targets**: The pure, the corrupt, the powerful
- **Payment**: **"Aether"** - Raw magical essence (real spellcasting fuel)
- **Risk**: Empire-level danger, might unleash abyssal horror
