## üåç CORE PREMISE: THE VEIL-SIGHT

**Player Character**: A survivor of a near-death experience with a permanent gift/curse: **The Veil-Sight**

**Ability**: Perceives two realities overlaid:
- **Human World**: Grimy, corrupt, dangerous metropolis
- **Dark World**: Supernatural entities, spirits, demons, fae

**Not Optional**: This is a constant, maddening state - player cannot turn it off.

### Initial Scene: "The Waking Nightmare"

**Location**: Back alley of St. Jude's Mercy Hospital

**Player Awakens**:
- Lying on wet cardboard
- Disoriented, head pounding
- Last memory: The event that "killed" them (mugging, corporate accident, overdose)

**Dual Reality**:

**First Conflict** (Tutorial):

2. Supernatural threat: Hungry Ghost attracted to player's life force
3. Solution: Make junkie walk through ghost, causing psychic attack, teaching cross-world mechanics

## üîã POWER SYSTEM: DUAL PROGRESSION

### Human World - "Path of Iron and Shadow"

**Skill Acquisition**:
- Find trainers (ex-soldier for firearms, hacker for net-running, fight club for combat)
- Skills are earned, not found

**Gear & Resources**:
- Black markets (gangs, cartels)
- Corporate espionage (steal prototypes)
- Crafting (safehouse workbenches)

**Influence & Reputation**:
- Track standing with factions
- High rep unlocks vendors, safehouses
- Being enemy triggers hit squads

### Dark World - "Path of Essence and Pacts"

**Essence Harvesting**:
1. Defeat supernatural entity
2. Form destabilizes, Essence bleeds out (10-15 seconds)
3. Use **Syphon Gauntlet** (channeled ability, 2-3 seconds, vulnerable)
4. Collect **Raw Essence** (different flavors per creature type)

**Progression Tiers**:

**Tier 1: Awakened** (Beginner)
- Powers raw, instinctual, uncontrollable
- Basic abilities (vampire life-drain, zombie slam)
- Requires small amounts of any Raw Essence ‚Üí Crude Essence

**Tier 2: Ascendant** (Intermediate)
- Control mastered, specialization begins
- Tactical abilities (vampire Mesmerize/Mist Form, zombie Grave Grasp/Corpse Detonation)
- Requires specific Essence types + rare Human World components

**Tier 3: Apex** (Master)
- Walking embodiment of archetype
- Ultimate abilities (vampire Lord of Night/Enthrall, zombie Plague-Bearer/Juggernaut Form)
- Requires vast refined Essence + unique catalyst items

**Soul Forging**:
- Must find **Locus of Power** (thin Veil locations: graveyards, haunted intersections, desecrated churches)
- Spend Essence to unlock/upgrade abilities

**Pacts & Artifacts**:
- **Pacts**: Fae Lord grants Glamour for future favor, Demon offers power for humanity piece
- **Artifacts**: Ghost locket for summoning, cursed grimoire for rituals

## üìñ MAIN STORYLINES

### 1. The Bleed (Main Quest)

**Premise**: Player's NDE was not random - they are a catalyst. Someone is intentionally weakening the Veil.

**Antagonist**: Veil-Breaker (human cult leader seeking lichdom OR powerful demon creating permanent gateway)

**Stakes**: Veridian City consumed by Dark World if Veil not stabilized

**Player Role**: Only person who can perceive full scope of threat

### 2. The Concrete Jungle & The Gilded Cage

**Premise**: Human corruption meets supernatural exploitation

**Factions**:
- **Aztecas Cartel**: Using crude necromancy to create "Husk" enforcers
- **OmniGen Corporation**: Capturing Fae, harvesting for drugs, creating changeling failures

**Player Choice**: Help one, destroy both, or play them against each other

### 3. The Courts of Dusk and Dawn

**Premise**: Fae civilization in exile due to Bleed

**Factions**:
- **Seelie (Court of Dawn)**: Lady Elara, sees humans as potential allies
- **Unseelie (Court of Dusk)**: Prince Malakor, sees humans as insects

**Gameplay**: Navigate treacherous politics, riddles, binding promises

**Player Choice**: Which Court dominates Veridian's supernatural underground

### 4. The Ghost in the Machine

**Premise**: Tech elite dying in impossible ways

**Antagonist**: **Techno-Geist** (vengeful programmer's spirit possessing networked tech)

**Setting**: Hunt through digital Dark World overlaid on server farms

**Resolution**: Appease or destroy

## üè¢ HUMAN WORLD FACTIONS (DETAILED)

### AeternaGen Corporation

**Description**: Tech/bio-genetics giant, secretly cult of science conquering death

**Leadership**: CEO Alistair Finch

**Goal**: Monopolize and weaponize Essence and Dark World

**Territory**: "The Spire" (financial district), AeternaGen Tower (fortress HQ with labs)

**Forces**: Corporate security, prototype Essence-Nullifier units

**Conflict**: Primary antagonist, hunts player, conflicts with everyone

### The Sombra Cartel

**Description**: International crime syndicate trading supernatural artifacts and Essence

**Leadership**: "La Mano" (shadowy council)

**Goal**: Profit from supernatural trade

**Territory**: Port District (shipyards, freight depots, trucking unions)

**Forces**: Smugglers, enforcers, occult specialists

**Conflict**: Primary rival to AeternaGen (trade vs control), uses street gangs as pawns

### The Chrome Skulls

**Description**: Violent street gang from industrial zone, masters of scavenging and jury-rigged tech

**Leadership**: "Scrap" (brutal leader)

**Territory**: "The Rustbelt" (abandoned factories, scrap yards)

**Forces**: Street fighters with improvised weapons and black-market cybernetics

**Conflict**: Battles Sombra Cartel for turf, raids AeternaGen convoys, chaotic neutral toward player

### The Praetorians (Corrupt Police)

**Description**: "Special Enforcement Unit" (SEU) - state-sanctioned mercenaries

**Leadership**: Commander Valerius

**Territory**: Jurisdiction anywhere, most active in Spire and Port

**Forces**: Heavily armed tactical units, APCs, drones

**Conflict**: For-hire muscle (AeternaGen retainer, Cartel bribes), hunts player if bounty placed

## üéØ IMPLEMENTATION IMPACT ON ARCHETYPE SYSTEM

### Current Architecture (7 Adapters Per Archetype):
1. personality
2. dialogue_style
3. action_policy
4. emotional_response
5. world_knowledge
6. social_dynamics
7. goal_prioritization

### Adjustments Needed:

**For Non-Speaking Creatures** (Zombies, Skeletons, basic Ghosts):
- **dialogue_style adapter**: Train on ambient sounds, moans, environmental audio
- **personality/social/goal adapters**: Simplified (hunger-driven, instinct-based)
- **Result**: Still 7 adapters, but some are minimal

**For Ambient/Environmental Creatures**:
- Can share base adapters across similar types
- Example: All "mindless undead" share base personality adapter
- Subfaction adapters for specific behaviors (skeleton vs ghoul movement)

**Resource Optimization**:
- **Gold-Tier** (dialogue required): 7 full adapters (~700MB each)
- **Silver-Tier** (limited dialogue): 7 adapters with shared bases (~400MB each)
- **Bronze-Tier** (ambient only): Shared adapters with minimal customization (~200MB each)
