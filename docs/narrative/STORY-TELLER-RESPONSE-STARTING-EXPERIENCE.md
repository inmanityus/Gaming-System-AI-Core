## ðŸ’€ DEATH SYSTEM: "Debt of Flesh"

### Mechanic 1: The Soul-Echo

Upon death:
- Perspective rips from body
- Become disembodied "Soul-Echo"
- Cannot interact
- Watch as **Corpse-Tender** emerges
- Corpse-Tender gathers body + ALL gear
- Drags it back to death location

### Mechanic 2: Re-Bodiment

- Soul dragged to last secured Hovel
- Painful reforming
- Awaken **naked, gasping**
- All gear GONE
- Afflicted with **"Veil-Fray"** (stacking debuff):
  - 1st death: -10% max Health/Stamina
  - 2nd consecutive: -25% max Health/Stamina
  - Continues stacking
  - **Removed by**: Successful corpse retrieval OR completing major deal

### Mechanic 3: The Corpse Run & The Bribe

**Your Corpse**:
- Remains as object at death location
- Contains ALL lost gear
- Guarded by: What killed you + Corpse-Tender

**Option A: The Naked Run** (High risk, high reward)
- Fight/sneak past dangers
- Retrieve gear manually
- No cost but very dangerous

**Option B: The Bribe** (Pay to pacify)
- Approach Corpse-Tender
- It desires **"Flesh Tithe"**
- Offer from stash:
  - Spare organ
  - Crafted drug
  - Rare Veil component
- **If sufficient**: Corpse-Tender pacifies hostile creatures temporarily
- Safe retrieval
- **Cost**: Steep economic consequence
